About Panimalar Engineering College

Panimalar Engineering College is a prestigious engineering institution located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in the year 2000, the college has grown into one of the leading engineering colleges in the region, with a student strength of over 6000+ students.

With a strong focus on academic excellence and student development, the college promotes innovation, leadership, and real-world learning through various technical and professional platforms.

  • Motto: Prosperity Endurance Character
  • Campus: Suburban | 250 Acres

What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is a simulation of the United Nations where students represent different countries and international organizations to debate global issues and propose solutions through diplomacy.

MUN builds essential life skills such as:

  • Public Speaking & Communication
  • Leadership & Teamwork
  • Negotiation & Diplomacy
  • Research & Policy Writing
  • Critical Thinking & Confidence

At PECMUN 2026, delegates will experience intense debate, structured procedures, and high-impact discussions.

About PECMUN 2026

PECMUN 2026 marks the inaugural Model United Nations conference organized by Panimalar Engineering College, Chennai.

This first edition is designed to provide a professional MUN experience with well-structured committees, impactful agendas, and an atmosphere that encourages delegates to think beyond boundaries.
