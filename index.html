About PEC MUN

Panimalar Engineering College’s Model United Nations

PEC MUN (Panimalar Engineering College Model United Nations) is the official Model United Nations conference organized by the MUN Club of Panimalar Engineering College, Chennai, functioning as a dynamic academic simulation of the United Nations where students assume the roles of global diplomats, policymakers, and international representatives to deliberate on pressing global challenges. Designed to provide an immersive diplomatic experience, PEC MUN enables delegates to represent countries, research assigned agendas, participate in structured committee debates, and collaboratively draft resolutions aimed at addressing real-world issues.
